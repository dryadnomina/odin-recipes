Cookies

Description

Simple plain cookies that will melt in your mouth!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup butter
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F
  2. In a small bowl, dissolve baking soda in milk
  3. In a seperate bowl, cream together the sugars and the egg.
  4. Then combine the oil, creamed butter, baking soda mixture, and cream of tartar
  5. Mix in flour and oats
  6. Allow for dough to chill in fridge for 1 hour
  7. Make small balls and flatten them with a greased or non-stick cookie sheet
  8. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes.
